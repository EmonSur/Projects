About Dungeon Dash

Play as a wizard, captured by his arch nemesis and trapped in a Dungeon. He must make it through room after room of deadly bats, all trying to get a turn to eat his flesh. This should be a piece of cake for our heroic wizard, but- Oh No! His powers are missing.

Here are some of the latest features added into the game:

  • Multiple levels, all with increasing difficulties.
  • Best time feature that will help you see how you stack up against previous tries.
  • Power-ups to help you beat the more difficult levels.
