Dungeon Dash Instructions

Welcome to Dungeon Dash, the ultimate test of your reflexes and gaming skills!

In this game, you will need to guide your player character across the canvas, dodging enemies and obstacles along the way. Here's how to play:

Gameplay

  • Press the up arrow once to make the player character jump.
  • If you manage to get to the other side of the canvas without colliding with an enemy, you win!
  • If you hit an enemy, you will be returned to the starting point and have to try again.
  • You have three lives before you lose.
  • The game has four levels, each with increasing numbers and speeds of enemies.
  • Each level has three difficulties: easy, medium, and hard.
  • The game is timed, and the fastest players for each level can make it onto the leaderboard.

Note: You may need to resize your window to fit the whole canvas. Please do so if need be.

Power ups

  • Ghost Mode (Press G): Ignores all collisions with enemies. Become invincible to enemies!
  • Time Freeze Mode (Press F): Freezes enemies in place. Stop enemies for a limited time!
  • Immortal Mode (Press I): Gives you unlimited lives. Never lose a life in this mode!
  • Assault Mode (Press A): Shoot enemies by pressing the space bar. Eliminate enemies with magic blasts!

You may enter as many modes as you want at the same time. Mix and match, it's up to you. To exit a mode, simply press the corresponding key again.

Good luck and have fun!
